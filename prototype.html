Built for pressure washing, detailing, lawn service, handyman, junk removal, and other local service businesses. Customers answer a few questions, get an instant quote or request an estimate, choose a time, sign the agreement, and the job lands in the contractor dashboard.
How it works
1. Contractor sets services
Add services, questions, pricing rules, or use estimate-only mode.
2. Send a branded link
Text or DM one link instead of going back and forth.
3. Customer accepts and schedules
Instant quote or in-person estimate request.
4. Agreement and saved job
Generate a clean confirmation and store the job in the dashboard.
