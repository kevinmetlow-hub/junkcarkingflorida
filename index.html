Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about selling your junk car in Florida.

How fast can I sell my junk car in South Florida? ▼
Fill out our form and get an instant cash offer in under 60 seconds. Once you accept, we schedule same-day or next-day pickup anywhere in South Florida — free towing always included.
Do you buy junk cars without a title in Florida? ▼
Yes — we buy cars with a clean title, registration only, or no documentation at all. Having a title gets you a higher offer, but we still purchase vehicles without one.
Is towing really free? ▼
100% free. Towing is always included with every purchase — no hidden fees, no deductions. We come to your location anywhere in Broward or Miami-Dade County.
How is my cash offer calculated? ▼
Your offer is based on year, make, model, mileage, condition, whether it runs, your documentation status, and catalytic converter. Our system uses current scrap and parts market values.
Do you buy cars that don't run? ▼
Absolutely. We buy cars in any condition — running, not running, flood damaged, wrecked, or sitting for years.
How do I get paid? ▼
Cash on the spot at pickup. No waiting for checks. When our driver picks up the car, you get paid immediately.
